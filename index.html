— FAQ Questions We
Hear Often.
How long does it take to see results? Most clients see measurable results within 30–60 days. This depends on your starting point, budget, and market. We set clear KPIs from day one so you always know where things stand.
Do you work with businesses outside of Asia? Yes. We operate from KL, Chiang Mai, and Da Nang but work with clients globally. All collaboration runs async via Slack, Notion, and Zoom — timezone friendly.
What is included in the free 30-min audit? We review your current brand, social media, website, and any active campaigns. You walk away with a clear view of what is working, what is not, and what to prioritise first.
Can I hire you for just one service? Absolutely. We offer branding, web development, social media, and paid campaigns as standalone services — not just full-service retainers.
How do you charge — retainer or project based? Both. Branding and web projects are fixed-scope. Ongoing services like social media management and paid campaigns run on a monthly retainer.
What makes The Beginners different from other agencies? Lean, senior-led team. No account managers in the middle. You work directly with the people doing the work — faster feedback loops, higher quality output.
